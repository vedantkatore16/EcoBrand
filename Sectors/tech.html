Case Studies

Carbon Reduction for a Cloud Services Company Challenge:

Challenge: A cloud services provider needed to reduce emissions from its energy-intensive data centers.

Solution: Implemented carbon accounting & transitioned company to renewable energy for data center operations.

Results: Reduced carbon emissions by 50% and positioned the company as a green leader in tech industry.

Ethical Sourcing for a Hardware Manufacturer Challenge:

Challenge:: A hardware manufacturer faced criticism for sourcing rare earth metals to unethical labor practices.

Solution: Conducted SCAs, introduced traceability systems, & ensured ESG compliance among suppliers.

Results: Improved supplier compliance by 40%, reduced reputational risks.

Circular Economy for an Electronics Company Challenge:

Challenge:An electronics firm faced increasing e-waste and consumer demand for sustainable practices.

Solution: Developed a take-back and recycling program & redesigned products for longevity.

Results: Reduced e-waste by 30% and saved $10 million in raw material costs.
