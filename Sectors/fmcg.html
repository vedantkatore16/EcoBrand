FMCG Sector

Overview

The fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) sector operates in a highly competitive market, facing growing scrutiny over its environmental and social impact. Planetary Plus provides tailored ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) solutions to help FMCG companies improve sustainability, enhance supply chain transparency, and build trust with stakeholders.

Case Studies

Waste Reduction for a Beverage Company

Challenge: :A beverage company faced criticism for its plastic waste and energy usage.

Solution: Implemented a waste reduction strategy, introducing reusable packaging in production.

Results: Reduced plastic waste by 30% and operational energy consumption by 20% , saved $10 million annually.

Sustainable Supply Chain Overhaul for a Food Brand

Challenge: A global food brand faced supply chain transparency issues linked to labor violations.

Solution: developed supplier codes of conduct and traceability systems, ensuring ESG compliance.

Results: Increased supplier compliance rates by 40% & strengthened partnerships with ESG-conscious retailers.

ESG Reporting for a Skincare Company

Challenge: A skincare company needed to attract investors while addressing environmental concerns.

Solution: Created ESG reports showcasing water conservation, reduced emissions, and ethical sourcing.

Results: Attracted $100 million in green investments and improved customer loyalty by 25%.
